# Bright-Car-Sales
This project analyses a car sales dataset to understand sales patterns, popular vehicles, customer demand, and revenue trends. The insights gained help a dealership make better decisions about stock, pricing, marketing, and overall sales strategy.

# Problem Statement

The dealership has accumulated detailed car sales records but does not have clear, actionable insight into sales performance, customer preferences, and revenue trends. Management needs to understand which vehicles sell the most, when sales peak or decline, which regions perform best, and how pricing influences demand. Without proper analysis, decisions about stock levels, marketing, and pricing are based on assumptions rather than evidence.

# Aim

To analyse historical car sales data to generate insights that improve sales strategy, inventory planning, and overall dealership performance.

# Objectives
- Analyse overall sales performance and trends.
- Identify peak and low sales periods.
- Determine which car brands and models drive the most sales.
- Understand how price affects sales volume.
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve sales and stock management.

# Steps Taken
- Brainstormed and planned the analysis approach.
- Processed and queried the dataset using SQL tools.
- Cleaned and prepared the data for analysis.
- Performed data analysis using SQL, Excel, and pivot tables.
- Created visualisations to identify patterns and trends.
- Presented insights and practical recommendations to stakeholders.
